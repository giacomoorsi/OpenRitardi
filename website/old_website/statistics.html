Regional differences

The map on the right shows that Italy faces strong regional differences in the average delay of trains for InterCity trains. In particular, the quality of that service is very low in the South of Italy. Regional trains show a good performance in the entire country, while FrecciaRossa high-speed trains have a surprisingly bad performance in the entire country.

You can use the dropdown below to select the train type you want to see on the map. By hovering with the mouse on a region, you can see the average delay of trains in that region and the number of trains registered in the 3-month period considered.
